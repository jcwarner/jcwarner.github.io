Chip Merkle

Chip Merkle was born in Jacksonville, Florida and is a fourth generation Floridian. He has been involved in many forms of ministry since 2000, some of them include music, evangelism, and church planting. He and his wife Natalie (also a Florida native), met in 1985 and have been married since 1989. They have 10 children.

Chip came to be known by the Lord in 1995 and has a passion for organic ministry. He and his wife have a heart for families.
