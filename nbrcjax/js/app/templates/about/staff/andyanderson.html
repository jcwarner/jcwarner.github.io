Welcome to New Berlin Road Church. My name is Andy Anderson. I have the awesome privilege and responsibility of being called as one of the Elders here at NBRC. I have been married to my beautiful wife, Miriam, for almost 20 years and we have 9 children.

At NBRC my responsibilities are to oversee the Nursery, Children's and Youth Ministries. As the primary teacher of the Youth, our focus is to grow deeper in our faith and knowledge of the word of God and put that faith into practice. In a world that is full of confusion and the lack of any absolute truth, we stand on the on true Word as our compass and guide to walk uprightly in the truth of the Holy Spirit.

Join us on Wednesday nights as we explore the depths of God's word, actively discuss the questions and struggles that we are all facing, and encourage each other to go deeper and strive for the finish line that is set before us.
