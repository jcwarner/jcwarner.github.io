Welcome!
New Berlin Road Church is a friendly, family oriented, down-to-earth, Holy Bible based congregation.

Armed with the truth of our God and His word, the Bible, we study scripture verse by verse in context. We believe God's word is sufficient to meet the needs in peoples lives today. We continually strive to develop faithful and fruitful followers of Jesus Christ.

If you have spiritual questions you would like answered, we will come alongside you to see what the Bible has to say on the matter.

We are part of the Southern Baptist Convention.

We welcome people of all ages and ethnicity. We desire to reach out with the same love with which Jesus Christ loved us.

As you browse through this site, it is our hope that you would consider joining us in person!
